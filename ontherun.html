HAVE YOU SEEN THIS MAN?

3 missing, 2 wounded. The Psycho Slicker leaves a trailblaze of tears, rage and cold hard cash as he rampages through the ice cut streets of Stockholm City. His last known whereabouts: Larsbodavägen 55, where dubious exchanges of a voluptuary nature were reportedly made.

Holding a mic to his latest living victim Gunnur Gretasson, we were told the following:

It was hell, pure blodig hell, the way he mocked and teased me. I was drinking with my girlfriends at Järntorgspumpen when he approached me. He told me I looked beautiful and flirted with me all through the night. We went back to his - a snuskig little hole on the outskirts of the city - and he offered me some stew with kroppkakor and meatballs. I was hungry - sue me! When I came to I was lying in my underwear by a Willys in Tyresö with 50 kronor in one hand and a pickle in the other. Please, don't make me recite this story any longer. Arrest this man and give him a taste of his own gryta!

CALL 020-5839-7206-4951 TO REPORT HIM NOW!

Women, particularly those in their 50s-70s, should be wary of the Slicker's seductive gambits. Whilst his movements have thus far proven erratic, Chief Deputy Tobbe of the Stockholm City constabulary has as of November 2022 dispatched over 70 officers to track the Slicker's tail.

C.D. Tobbe had this to say on the ongoing case:

I've dealt with these dog types a million times. Big loads, heavy armour. But when you strip 'em down? Butt naked underneath. I mean pornographic, emperor's new clothes, as-the-day-you-were-born naked. When you realise that shit? That's when you know there's nothing to worry about. Next!

To the average man or woman on the street, the Psycho Slicker's motives are at best disconcerting. But thanks to the efforts of the constabulary unit, the Slicker will God willing be incarcerated before Christmas.
